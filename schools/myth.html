Balance

Myth PVP in a nutshell

As a myth your strategy is all about keeping your opponent in a stun lock, and therefor not having to worry about what they will do

Myth can use nasty combos such as medusa into a feint, into a shadow hit

Spell List

  1. Myth blade
  2. Stun
  3. Medusa
  4. Feint
  5. Shatter
  6. Any Shadow Hit

Strategy

  1. Blade up
  2. Medusa
  3. Shatter or feint, depending on the situation
  4. Go for the kill
  5. If this fails, cry
