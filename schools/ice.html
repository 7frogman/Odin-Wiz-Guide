Ice

Ice PVP in a nutshell

If you PVP with an Ice wizard you will feel like a god, in fact you might become a semigod.

As long as you use the meta gear you will have so much health that your opponents will cry, yet when trading blows you will hit your opponent just as hard as they hit you.

If you lose a single match using this guide, either you haven't followed it or you're too foolish for this game

Spell List

  • Ice Blade
  • Sharpened Blade
  • Any Shadow Hit
  • Frost Beetle
  • Carry shields, alt strat only
  • Carry Satyr, alt strat only

Strategy

  1. Blade
  2. Blade again
  3. Blade until you get a shadow pip
  4. Kill them

Alternative Strategy

  1. Blade
  2. Blade again
  3. Shield just when they are about to hit you, bonus if you use a set shield
  4. Right after they hit, use satyr to destroy their mind
  5. Go back to step one
